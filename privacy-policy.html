Privacy Policy for Notion Palette Pro

Effective date: February 23, 2026

Home Page: https://github.com/SahilFruitwala/NotionPalettePro

Notion Palette Pro is a browser extension that lets users apply visual themes to Notion. This policy describes what data the extension handles.

Information We Collect

Notion Palette Pro does not collect personal information, account credentials, browsing history, or page content.

Information Stored by the Extension

The extension stores one setting in Chrome storage: your selected theme name (for example, "nord" or "dracula"), so your preference persists between sessions.

How Information Is Used

The stored theme preference is used only to apply your selected style in Notion.

Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Remote Connections

The extension does not transmit user data to our servers. The popup may load a web font from Google Fonts for UI styling.

Data Retention and Deletion

The stored theme preference remains in your browser storage until you change it, clear browser data, or uninstall the extension.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact the publisher through the Chrome Web Store listing.
